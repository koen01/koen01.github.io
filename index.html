Things I've built

→ 001
CFSync Web WebSocket 3D printing

A local web dashboard for managing filament spools on Creality K1-series printers with the CFS unit (K1, K1C, K1 Max, K1 SE, K2 Plus). Connects directly to the printer over WebSocket, reads live spool data from every CFS slot, and optionally syncs consumption back to Spoolman.

 → 002
CFtag Android RFID 3D printing

An Android app for reading and writing Creality RFID filament spool tags. Pairs with CFSync and Spoolman to give you a seamless, end-to-end filament tracking workflow — from the spool in your hand to the slot in the printer.

 → 003
Moonraker Viewer Flutter Moonraker Read-only

A super-simple Flutter app that puts your 3D printer's camera feed fullscreen with live print info overlaid — landscape mode, pinch-to-zoom, no buttons to press. The lazy person's print monitor, sitting on a stand by the bench.
